Oak and beech furniture board.

Solid oak boards are available in following dimensions and classes:

  • dimensions: thickness 18-45 mm, width up to 1300 mm, length up to 3000 mm
  • class: A/B, B/C, C/C, D/D, Rustic

Oak boards with micro joints are available in following dimensions and classes:

  • dimensions: thickness 18-45 mm, width up to 1300 mm, length up to 5000 mm
  • class: A/B, B/C, C/C, D/D, Rustic

Solid beech boards are available in following dimensions and classes:

  • dimensions: thickness 18-45 mm, width up to 1300 mm, length up to 3000 mm
  • class: A/B, B/C, C/C, D/D

Beech boards with micro joints are available in following dimensions and classes:

  • dimensions: thickness 18-45 mm, width up to 1300 mm, length up to 5000 mm
  • class: A/B, B/C, C/C, D/D

Moisture content of the boards: 8% ± 2%

Boards are ground according to the customer's expectations: P-80, P-100, P-120, P-150

The first order is completed within 30-90 days, the next ones are completed according to the order schedule.

In order to prepare a trade offer with the price, please specify the type, class and quantity for which we are to prepare an offer. We always present the offer as full truck deliveries, 26-28 m3 for one transport.

When ordering a smaller quantity, the delivery costs do not decrease and thus the cost of the product increases accordingly, which may translate into differences between the price submitted in the offer and the price included in the contract.
