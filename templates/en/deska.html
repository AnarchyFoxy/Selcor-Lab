Oak and beech planks.

 

We are a supplier of unpruned oak and beech wood, wet and dry in classes I, II, III and MIX.

So far, we have been completing orders for Customers from Europe and China, but we are ready to deliver the offered products to any place indicated by the Customer.

Lumber comes from trees growing in eastern Poland and Ukraine.

The most commonly used thicknesses of the lumber shipped are between 26 mm and 50 mm, however, we can supply other thicknesses tailored to customer requirements.

In order to prepare a trade offer with the price, please specify the type, class and quantity for which we are to prepare an offer. We always present the offer as full truck deliveries, with one transport.

When ordering a smaller quantity, the delivery costs do not decrease and thus the cost of the product increases accordingly, which may translate into differences between the price submitted in the offer and the price included in the contract.
