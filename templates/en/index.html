About us

Our company was registered in the National Court Register on 19 November 2007 and has been continuously operating since then.

Our commercial activity is in the field of trade in solid wood panels and planks, friezes and slats made of natural wood.

Our experience of more than ten years is at the same time a motivation for further development, closer cooperation with current Clients and gaining the trust of new Contractors.
